Here I make available the results of my investigations and expert analyses, my interviews and legal actions, and links and explanations regarding the source documents of the clinical trials.

You will find here my "Evaluation of the methodological practices implemented in the Pfizer trials in the development of its messenger RNA vaccine against COVID-19 with regard to Good Clinical Practice." This report highlights the multiple methodological biases that invalidate the results of the phase 3 clinical trial that led to the real-world use of the Comirnaty® vaccine.

My conclusions explain our reality today:
- poor efficacy of the vaccine
- limited duration of protection
- numerous and worrying serious side effects!

The work of these recent years has become a book, *"Tous vaccinés, tous protégés ?"* ("All vaccinated, all protected?"), published by Guy Tredaniel.

## My Book
A book about vaccines, the serious failings of the COVID clinical trials, and above all, a collection of testimonies from the victims.
