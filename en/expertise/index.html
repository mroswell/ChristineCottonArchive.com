Expertise & Reports

The heart of Christine Cotton's work: a Good Clinical Practice (GCP) critique of the Pfizer/BioNTech pivotal vaccine trial C4591001. The report exists in four editions, grew over three years, and is supplemented by her OPECST parliamentary testimony and the slides she presented to Dr Peter McCullough.

Her flagship 414-page expert report assessing Good Clinical Practice, efficacy, safety and immunogenicity across every Pfizer trial population — adults, adolescents, children, boosters, bivalents, pregnancy.
