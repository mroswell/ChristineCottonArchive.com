The book

Christine Cotton's book exists in two editions: a French print edition, Tous vaccinés, tous protégés ? ("All vaccinated, all protected?"), published by Éditions Guy Trédaniel; and a free English ebook, The Trial Was Almost Perfect.

French print edition

Tous vaccinés, tous protégés ?
Éditions Guy Trédaniel, 2023

A book about the vaccines, the failings of the COVID clinical trials, and a collection of testimonies from victims. Preface by Michèle Rivasi (MEP), co-written with Senator Laurence Muller-Bronn; foreword by Virginie Joron (MEP) and Martine Wonner (former Member of Parliament).
