# Christine Cotton — Archive

A local archive of **christinecotton.com**, the personal site of Christine Cotton (b. 27 April 1970), a French biostatistician and founder of the CRO **Statitec**, who became a whistleblower with a detailed Good Clinical Practice (GCP) critique of Pfizer/BioNTech's COVID-19 vaccine clinical trials.

## Start here
Open **`catalog/index.html`** in a browser. It is the searchable table of contents of everything, filterable by **document type**, **tag**, and **language**, with links to the local archive and the live originals.

- **`catalog/index.html`** — master table of contents (39 items).
- **`catalog/interviews.html`** — her 18 video interviews (titles + channels + links; searchable). Transcripts not yet generated.
- **`catalog/external_links.html`** — 231 external links/sources she cited, grouped by category with one-line descriptions, searchable.

## Folder layout
- `archive/pages/` — every site page, French original, with navigation boilerplate stripped.
- `archive/pages_en/` — English translations of the French-only pages (home, criticism, legal notice, book sources).
- `archive/documents/` — full extracted text of her key documents:
  - `CV_Christine_Cotton_EN.md` — her complete English CV.
  - `Expertise_2024-12-30_English_full.md` — her flagship 414-page English report (full text).

## Translation note
Christine Cotton translated most of her major works into English herself. Where an English edition exists (the book, the 414/105/54/35-page reports), the archive uses or links to *her own* English version rather than a re-translation. The few French-only pages were translated and placed in `archive/pages_en/`.

## Known limitations
- **Binary files** (the raw PDFs and images) could not be downloaded directly from this environment; the catalog links to the live originals so you can download them.
- A few documents (the 35-page and 2021 summaries) are **image-based PDFs** with no machine-readable text, so only the live links are provided.
- The **book's full prose** is a commercial work not published on the site; an English ebook is available via her Support page (linked in the catalog).
